About me

With a strong foundation in engineering, I pursued further studies in Software Analysis and Development, where I gained expertise in SQL, Python, and Power BI. My technical background allows me to combine analytical thinking with problem-solving skills to deliver impactful solutions.

My career reflects a unique blend of disciplines: the precision and rigor of engineering together with the innovation and agility of IT.

Driven by curiosity and continuous learning, I thrive in multidisciplinary environments where I can leverage my engineering mindset and technical skills to optimize processes, build scalable solutions, and create real business value.
